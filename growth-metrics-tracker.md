## TOOLS FOR TRACKING

### Analytics Stack
- **Google Analytics 4**: Traffic, conversions, user behavior
- **Mixpanel**: Product usage, feature adoption
- **Stripe Dashboard**: Revenue, subscriptions, churn
- **ConvertKit**: Email performance, automation metrics
- **LinkedIn Campaign Manager**: Ad performance
- **Google Ads**: Search campaign metrics

### Reporting Schedule
- **Daily**: Revenue, trials, key metrics check
- **Weekly**: Full dashboard review, channel performance
- **Monthly**: Comprehensive analysis, strategy adjustment
- **Quarterly**: Deep dive, forecast adjustment

This tracking framework ensures we maintain visibility into all aspects of growth while staying focused on the $4,900/month revenue target.

## ðŸ“ž INTERVIEW SCRIPT

### Opening (1-2 minutes)
```
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to chat! 

As I mentioned, we're building an AI chatbot platform specifically for businesses like yours, and I'd love to learn about your current customer service challenges.

This is really a learning conversation for us - no pitch, just want to understand your world better. Sound good?"
```

### Discovery Questions (8-10 minutes)
1. **Current Process**: "Walk me through how you currently handle customer inquiries"
2. **Pain Points**: "What's the most frustrating part of your customer service process?"
3. **Volume**: "How many customer questions do you get per day/week?"
4. **Repetition**: "What percentage would you say are repetitive or similar questions?"
5. **Cost**: "How much time does your team spend on customer service daily?"
6. **Tools**: "What tools are you currently using for customer service?"
7. **Automation**: "Have you tried any automation before? What was that experience like?"

### Solution Validation (3-4 minutes)
8. **Interest**: "If you could automate 80% of repetitive questions, what would that mean for your business?"
9. **Features**: "What would be most important in an AI chatbot solution?"
10. **Pricing**: "What would you expect to pay monthly for a solution like this?"
11. **Timeline**: "If this existed today, how quickly would you want to implement it?"

### Closing (1 minute)
```
"This has been super helpful! Based on what you've shared, I think our solution could be a great fit.

Would you be interested in being one of our beta customers? We're looking for 10 businesses to try it free for 30 days and give us feedback.

No commitment - just want to see if it solves your problems. Interested?"
```
